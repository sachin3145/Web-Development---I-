MAKING A BURGER

  1. Start by taking two slices of bread from the burger bun.
  2. Put patty on one slice of bread.
  3. Add any number of toppings you like on the patty (Cheese, Lettuce, Tomato etc.).
  4. Place another slice of bread on top of the patty with the cheese slice facing downwards.
  5. Enjoy!
